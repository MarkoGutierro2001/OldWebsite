Midterm Project - War

Description: With this project I was able to create anything I desired. The only reqirement was that I had to utilize the skills I had learned throughout the year up to the current point. I chose to recreate the classic card game of War.

K - What I knew | L - What I learned | W - What I want to Learn

K - I knew how to design user interfaces, write event-driven programs, and update variables.
L - I learned how to make effective comments. I needed them in order to keep everything organized.
W - I want to learn how to make a game where you can verse someone who is not in the same room.
