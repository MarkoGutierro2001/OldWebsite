Trivial Pursuit App

Description: The purpose of this project was to create a multi-screen app. I decided to create a trivia app on Youtube's most subscribed content creator, Felix Kjellberg. He is also known by his channel name which is Pewdiepie.

K - What I knew | L - What I learned | W - What I want to Learn

K - I knew how to make programs that do not go through any change (The outcome is always the same).
L - I learned how to design user interfaces and how to write event-driven programs.
W - I want to learn how to put my app on the app store for other people to play.
